About Us

Lost Paws is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals in need. Our mission is to provide shelter, care, and love to abandoned and stray animals, giving them a second chance at life.

Our Services

At Lost Paws, we offer a range of services to support animals in need, including veterinary care, fostering, adoption programs, and community outreach initiatives. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure the welfare and well-being of every animal we serve.

Adopt a Pet

Looking to add a furry friend to your family? Browse our available animals for adoption and give a loving home to a pet in need. Adopting from Lost Paws not only changes the life of an animal but also enriches your own.
